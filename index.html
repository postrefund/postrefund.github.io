How PostRefund Works

Did you know that you are entitled to a refund if your Canada Post shipment is late?

It can be a time-consuming process to manually keep track of all your shipments and claim potential refunds. PostRefund integrates into your Shopify store and fully automates the refund process.

Who is this service for

Your store ships with Canada Post, and you don't like paying for late shipments. In the future we will add additional e-commerce platforms and shipping providers, please let us know what you options you would like to see integrated.

Case study

Elmwood Electronics has been using PostRefund technology for over 6 months on their Shopify store. In that time, they have recovered over $3,000 in Canada Post shipping fees, or about 9% of their total spend! We have taken our lessons learned from working with Elmwood Electronics' Shopify store and built it into the best Shopify shipping refund app!

Pricing

Your first 10 refund requests are free and then we charge you C$2.50 for each refund requested.
