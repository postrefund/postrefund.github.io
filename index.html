How PostRefund Works

Did you know that you are entitled to a refund if your Canada Post shipment was late?

Currently it's a time consuming process to manually keep track of all your shipments and claim potential refunds. PostRefund integrates to your Shopify shop and keeps track of your shipments automatically. When a late shipment is detected, we send you an email and with the click of one button you can claim the refund. We will soon have this fully automated.

Who is this service for

You are using Shopify to sell goods and Canada Post to fulfill shipments. In the future we will add additional options (e-commerce solutions and shipping companies) to our offering, please let us know what you options you would like to see integrated.

Pricing

Our service is free as long as it is in private beta. After we launch we will give you 25 automatic refund requests for free and then charge you US$1 for each refund claimed.
